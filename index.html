A Bit About Me

Pursuing a certificate in coding has given me the outlet I have been seeking to make a personal evolution into an area where I can grow and find a footing in a new career that pairs well with my education. Currently I enjoy living in the St. Louis area with my family, and silver lab named Vegas. I have been a full-time stay-at-home parent for nearly 17 years and before that I received my BFA in Studio Art from The University of Texas at Austin, and a MS in Counseling Psychology from Tarleton State University. For fun I like gaming and playing hockey. Moslty, I love to learn new things, and I am excited to have the opportunity to gain technological skills that create potential to connect with people and improve lives in creative ways!
